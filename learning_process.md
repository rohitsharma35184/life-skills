## 1. How to Learn Faster with the Feynman Technique

### Question 1  
### What is the Feynman Technique? Explain in 1 line.

The Feynman Technique is a method of learning where you try to explain a topic in simple words and then identify the parts you do not understand clearly.

### Question 3  
### What are active and diffused modes of thinking?

Active mode of thinking is when we are fully focused and concentrating on a task.  
Diffused mode of thinking is when the brain is relaxed but still working in the background.

When Dali relaxed and almost fell asleep, he entered diffused mode.  
As soon as he woke up, his brain shifted back to active mode. This combination helped him think creatively and solve problems better.

## 3. Learn Anything in 20 Minutes

### Question 4  
### What are the steps to take when approaching a new topic? (Only mention the points)

1. Break the skill into small and important parts.  
2. Learn enough to understand the mistakes you are making.  
3. Remove distractions and stay aware of what you are learning.  
4. Practice for at least 20 hours and commit to learning before starting.
