# Grit and Growth Mindset — Assignment

## 1. Grit — Summary

Grit means having strong dedication and not giving up easily. It is about working continuously towards your long-term goals. Sometimes things become difficult and progress is slow, but if we stay consistent and keep trying, we can achieve success. Grit is about patience, hard work, and not quitting when problems come.

---

## 2. Introduction to Growth Mindset — Summary

Growth mindset means believing that we can improve our skills and intelligence with practice. It means we should not think that we are bad at something permanently. Instead, we should believe that with effort and learning, we can become better. People with a growth mindset accept challenges and learn from their mistakes instead of feeling discouraged.

---

## 3. Internal Locus of Control

### What is Internal Locus of Control?

Internal locus of control means believing that our success or failure depends mainly on our own actions and efforts. It means we should not blame luck, situations, or other people for everything.

### Important Point

To stay motivated, we should focus on things that are in our control like our effort, habits, discipline, and attitude. Instead of complaining about problems, we should take responsibility and try to improve ourselves.

---

## 4. Key Points to Build a Growth Mindset

- Believe that skills can improve with practice  
- Accept challenges instead of avoiding them  
- Learn from mistakes  
- Do not give up after failure  
- Focus more on effort than only results  
- Keep learning new skills  
- Replace negative thinking with positive learning thoughts  
- Accept feedback and try to improve  
- Step out of your comfort zone  
- Practice regularly and stay consistent
